Short Bio

Werley Cordeiro has been an Economist at Sicredi Asset Management since August 2024. Previously, he worked as a Data Analyst at Itaú from 2022 to 2024. In early 2022, he completed a four-month PhD summer internship in the Data Science team at Itaú Asset Management. Werley holds a PhD in Economics from the Federal University of Santa Catarina, Brazil (2023).
